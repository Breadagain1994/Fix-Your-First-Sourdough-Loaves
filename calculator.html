👋 Hi, I’m Tjard — and this is Breadagain.

Three years ago, a colleague handed me a living, bubbling jar of wild yeast. It was a sourdough starter born in 2016, named Black Death (yes, really). I had no idea what I was doing — but I was instantly hooked.

My first bake was Joe Semola's "Easy Peasy Sourdough Bread." It wasn't perfect, but it was mine. That feeling of creating something real — with my hands, from simple ingredients — changed everything for me. I call it BIY: Bake It Yourself. Sourdough became my way to slow down, create, experiment, and have fun — even when the loaves turned out weird.

🍞 My Style

I love working with a mix of wheat, rye, and spelt, typically around 75% hydration — and yes, I still remember my first game-changer: chocolate sourdough. That was the moment it really clicked. I'm not a trained baker — just a home baker figuring things out as I go.

📒 Why I Made This Planner

When I started, I really wished something like this existed. A place to track my starter, understand hydration, avoid overproofed disasters, and repeat successful bakes.

So I created it — for beginners, for home bakers, and for anyone trying to figure it out loaf by loaf. This planner comes from experience — not perfection. Use it however you like, adapt it to your rhythm, and most importantly: enjoy the process.
