Do I need to be a professional baker?

No. The framework is designed specifically for home bakers who want to eliminate the unpredictability of manual recipes by using strict data logs instead.

Is the tracker physical or digital?

The Consistency System is a high-quality physical tool meant to be stored in your kitchen laboratory. Digital systems are impractical when working with high-hydration doughs on your hands.

Can I use this with rye or spelt flour?

Yes. The architectural tracking logs allow you to record the specific protein and ash content of any flour variable to map its unique degradation curve.
